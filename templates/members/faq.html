Whay are posts limited to Areas of Study?

Studies on Chinese social media suggest that international students are increasingly looking to STEM (Science, Technology. Engineering, Maths) and Arts subjects for further study. The use of study based social media posts better demonstrate Australia's worldclass Australian education. Furthermore, for these students where English is not their native language, no matter their IELTS or TOEFL scores, Area of Study interests transcends this communication challenge.

Why a short 140 character post limit?

Directed and factual posts at 100 words triggers an instantaneous response to associate personally with the message. This leads to higher Enlingo profile views and potential sales enquires.

What are Enlingo Credits used for?

Credits are used to offset the unique views to your Enlingo profile. Put simply, our technology equates a profile view to credit ratio of 1:1. Different packages are priced differently according to how our affiliate network charges Enlingo. We currently cap our rates at $0.02 AUD per credit.
